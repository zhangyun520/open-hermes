# Human Transition Layer / 人类安置层

不能用降低 `D_task` 的方式，制造更大的 `D_human`。

Automation must consider the jobs, income, identity, skills, dignity, and transition paths of affected people.

## Principles

- 先增强，再替代。
- 先转岗，再裁撤。
- 先分润，再吸收经验。
- 先安置，再规模化自动化。

## Required Questions

Before scaling automation, ask:

1. Which human tasks are being compressed?
2. Who loses income, status, or learning opportunities?
3. What skills remain human?
4. What transition paths exist?
5. Is human review required?
6. Does the automation create a larger social residual?

## Human Transition Card

A Human Transition Card should record automated tasks, remaining human tasks, displacement risk, retraining needs, dignity risk, and approval requirements.
