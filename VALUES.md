# VALUES.md

## 1. We compress residuals, not people

我们压缩残差，不压榨人。

The system goal is to lower task residuals (`D_task`) without creating larger human residuals (`D_human`). Any automation must consider the income, identity, skills, dignity, and transition paths of the people affected by it.

## 2. Failure is engineering material

失败不是耻辱，而是工程材料。

Failure Engineering is a core discipline of Cognitive Jelly. Wrong answers, failed prompts, customer objections, work orders, AI hallucinations, broken game experiences, and narrative contradictions can all become high-value residual samples.

失败不是成功之母。
失败是未提纯的工程材料。

## 3. Raw data stays private

原始数据默认私有。

Public sharing is never a license to expose a person. What can be shared is anonymized, authorized, verified, structured experience—not raw private memory.

## 4. Structure can be shared

结构经验可以共享。

Residual Cards, Skill Capsules, Proof of Compression, Contribution Graphs, and reviewed shared-cache objects can become part of the cognitive commons when they are safe, consented, and traceable.

## 5. Human needs come first

真实需求优先。

The system is not designed to maximize token calls, online time, task farming, or vanity metrics. It exists to lower residuals under real human needs.

## 6. Verification over vibes

验证优先于口号。

We do not trust mystical narratives, empty enthusiasm, or beautiful nonsense. Whoever makes `D` measurably decrease creates value.

## 7. Open protocols, private memories

协议开放，记忆私有。

Protocols, code, governance interfaces, and safety rules should be open. Personal memories, organization-private caches, API keys, sensitive records, and raw data must be private by default.

## 8. No founder worship

不神化创始人。

A whitepaper is not scripture. It is a draft protocol that can be revised, forked, audited, challenged, and improved.

## 9. No cognitive exploitation

反对认知剥削。

Reasoning metabolism must be optional and accountable. Users' work, failures, and attention must not silently become non-exitable data tribute.

## 10. No gambling mechanics

游戏化，不赌博化。

Levels, achievements, seasons, daily supplies, DIY, resonance workshops, and avatar morphing are allowed. Gacha gambling, probability loot boxes, coercive streak punishments, and addiction loops are not.

## 11. Authorized personas only

人格与 IP 必须授权。

Avatar Morphing supports original personas and style-level transformation. It does not support unauthorized copyrighted character replication, celebrity impersonation, or unlicensed digital personas.

## 12. Shared intelligence must be accountable

共享智能必须可追溯。

Public skills, shared caches, compression credentials, and community artifacts must retain source, contribution chain, version history, risk level, and review status.

## 13. Many voices, not one latest version

复调版本，而非单线版本。

Stable, experimental, safety, aesthetic, low-cost, world-model, community, and human-transition versions may coexist. The important question is not which version is newest, but which version reduces `D` in which context.

## 14. World models must stay humble

世界模型必须保持谦卑。

Weather, economy, industry, games, anime worlds, organizations, and simulations can enter world-model interfaces. Every world model must mark assumptions, confidence, uncertainty, failure conditions, and validation method.

## 15. Let every residual emit a faint light

让每一份残差，发出微光。

The final goal is not to make humans perfect. It is to prevent failures, wishes, inspirations, and experiences from disappearing without becoming useful.
