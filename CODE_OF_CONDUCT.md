# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md

## Our Pledge

We build Cognitive Jelly as a cognitive commons. Contributors should be treated with dignity, especially when they report failures, uncertainty, confusion, or mistakes.

## Expected Behavior

- Respect contributors and reviewers.
- Welcome new contributors and explain context patiently.
- Encourage honest failure reports.
- Repair system bias when found.
- Credit contributors without turning contribution records into moral rankings.
- Protect privacy, consent, and attribution.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Shaming people for failure or lack of expertise.
- Attacking newcomers.
- Using cognitive fingerprints for identity discrimination.
- Uploading another person's private data.
- Submitting unauthorized IP, personas, or copyrighted character copies.
- Encouraging gambling mechanics or addiction loops.
- Treating the project as a religion or founder cult.
- Treating the contribution ledger as merit judgment or moral debt.
- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or doxxing.

## Enforcement

Maintainers and moderators may remove content, request changes, quarantine artifacts, or restrict participation when behavior threatens safety, privacy, legality, or the cognitive commons.
